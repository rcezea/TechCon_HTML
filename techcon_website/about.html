Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate technologists. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the most influential global technology conferences, attracting thousands of attendees from around the world each year.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the global tech community by creating a platform where knowledge, innovation, and diversity thrive. We aim to inspire the next generation of leaders, developers, and creators.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lin

Dr. Ada Lin is a leading voice in AI ethics and policy. At TechCon 2022, she spoke on the societal impact of large language models and the importance of ethical frameworks in tech innovation.

Marcus Reid

Marcus is the founder of OpenDev, an open-source platform empowering developers around the globe. His keynote at TechCon 2021 inspired many to contribute to open-source communities.

Priya Natarajan

A pioneer in distributed systems, Priya introduced breakthrough ideas on scalable cloud infrastructure during her TechCon 2020 appearance.
